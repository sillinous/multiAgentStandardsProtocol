## Executive Summary

The SuperStandard Consortium offers five membership tiers designed to accommodate organizations and individuals of all sizes. From global enterprises to individual developers, everyone can participate in shaping the future of multi-agent systems standards.

**Membership Philosophy:**
- **Inclusive**: Open to all who want to contribute
- **Fair**: Benefits scaled to investment level
- **Sustainable**: Dues support consortium operations
- **Meritocratic**: Technical influence based on contribution, not payment

---

| Tier | Annual Dues | Seats | Technical Vote | Business Vote | Certification |
|------|-------------|-------|----------------|---------------|---------------|

## 8. Membership Lifecycle

### 8.1 Joining Process

**Steps:**
1. Select membership tier
2. Submit online application
3. Receive Membership Agreement
4. Execute agreement and pay dues
5. Onboarding by member services team
6. Activate benefits

**Timeline:** 5-10 business days from payment

### 8.2 Annual Renewal

**Process:**
- 60-day renewal notice sent
- Review and update member information
- Renew agreement and pay dues
- Auto-renew option available

**Grace Period:** 30 days after expiration

### 8.3 Upgrades & Downgrades

**Mid-Year Upgrade:**
- Pay prorated difference
- Benefits activate immediately

**Mid-Year Downgrade:**
- Effective at next renewal
- No refund for current year

### 8.4 Termination

**Member-Initiated:**
- 30-day written notice
- No refund for current year
- All benefits cease at end of notice period

**Consortium-Initiated:**
- For cause (Code of Conduct violations, non-payment)
- 30-day cure period for non-payment
- Appeals to Governing Board

## 9. Fees & Payment

### 9.1 Annual Dues

- **Payment Schedule**: Annual, paid in advance
- **Invoicing**: Net 30 payment terms
- **Methods**: Wire transfer, ACH, credit card (< $25K)
- **Currency**: USD (other currencies accepted with conversion fees)

### 9.2 Fee Adjustments

**Annual CPI Adjustment:**
- Dues may increase by Consumer Price Index annually
- Max 5% increase per year
- 90-day notice provided

**Discounts:**
- Founding member: 50% off first year (first 20 members)
- Multi-year commitment: 10% off (3+ years)
- Volume (multiple entities): Negotiable

### 9.3 Refund Policy

- No refunds for membership dues
- Exception: Consortium dissolution (pro-rated refund)
- Certification fees: Refundable if not started

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can a company have multiple membership tiers?**  
A: No, one tier per legal entity. Subsidiaries may join separately.

**Q: Do membership benefits transfer if acquired?**  
A: Governing Board reviews. Generally, yes, if membership agreement transferred.

**Q: Can we share our membership across divisions?**  
A: Yes, all employees of member organization benefit.

**Q: What happens to our Board seat if we downgrade?**  
A: Platinum seats lost immediately. Class representatives serve out term.

**Q: Can academics commercialize research?**  
A: Requires commercial membership and certification for commercial products.

**Q: How do individual contributors earn recognition?**  
A: Through technical contributions. Top contributors featured quarterly.

**Q: Can we try before joining?**  
A: Yes, attend public meetings, join mailing lists, review specs on GitHub.

**Q: Are there regional/local chapters?**  
A: Planned for future. Currently one global consortium.
